About Levene Energy

An integrated energy group built for Africa

Levene Energies Limited Limited is a Nigerian energy conglomerate with a diversified portfolio spanning oil and gas exploration, energy trading, renewable energy deployment, and infrastructure development.

Through our component entities, we provide innovative, technology-led solutions across the entire value chain empowering businesses, communities, and the Nigerian energy landscape with a commitment to excellence and sustainability.

Headquartered in Lagos, with offices in Abuja, London and Mauritius, Levene operates with global standards and local expertise.
