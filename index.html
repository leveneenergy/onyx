About Levene Energies

Integrated. Diversified. Built for Africa.

Levene Energies Limited is a diversified energy conglomerate with operations spanning natural gas infrastructure, crude oil and refined products trading, renewable energy solutions, and upstream engineering services.

Through our component entities, we provide innovative, technology-led solutions across the entire value chain by empowering businesses, communities, and the Nigerian energy landscape with a commitment to excellence and sustainability.

With offices in Abuja, Lagos, London, Mauritius and Dubai, our business is guided by quality, professionalism and a commitment to excellence.
